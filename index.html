Vrinda Store Annual Sales Report 2022

Tools Excel

The Vrinda Store Annual Sales Report for 2022 provides a comprehensive overview of sales and order performance across multiple dimensions. Monthly sales peaked in March and gradually declined toward the end of the year. Women accounted for 64% of total sales, indicating a predominantly female customer base. The majority of orders (92%) were successfully delivered, with minimal cancellations, returns, or refunds. Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh emerged as the top-performing states, while Amazon and Myntra were the leading sales channels. Analysis by age and gender shows that adult women placed the highest number of orders, highlighting the importance of targeting this demographic. Overall, the report reflects strong online sales performance, efficient order fulfillment, and clear insights into customer behavior.
